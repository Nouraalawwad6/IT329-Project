Healthy Hot Chocolate

Recipe Creator

Sara Ahmed

Category

Hot Drinks

Description

A simple and healthy hot chocolate made with natural ingredients and no refined sugar. Perfect for cozy winter moments.

Ingredients

  • 1.5 cups milk of choice
  • 1/2 scoop chocolate protein powder (≈ 15-18 g)
  • Honey or sweetener of choice — 1 tbsp
  • 1/4 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • A pinch of sea salt

Instructions

  1. Heat the milk in a pan over medium heat until it begins to gently simmer.
  2. Whisk in the cocoa powder and protein powder until fully dissolved.
  3. Remove from heat.
  4. Stir in the vanilla extract and honey (or sweetener of choice).
  5. Froth the mixture or whisk by hand, then serve and enjoy.
